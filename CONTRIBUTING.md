# Contributing to Enigma Sound
Thank you for your interest in the Enigma Sound research project! I am currently scaling this architecture to bridge the gap between Affective Computing and production-level AI.

As the primary maintainer, I welcome collaborators who are passionate about the intersection of AI, emotion, and music.

### 🛠 How You Can Help:
-Vertex AI Migration: I am looking to refactor custom LSTM layers to Google Vertex AI. 

-Gemini Embeddings: I plan to implement Google Gemini’s multimodal embeddings for deeper emotional nuance and need help testing the integration.

-Edge Optimization: If you are a Flutter expert, help me optimize the frontend for mid-range mobile devices.

### Getting Started:
1. Fork the repo and create your branch.

2. Check the Issues tab for tasks labeled good first issue.

3. Submit a Pull Request (PR) with a clear description of your changes. I review all PRs personally.
